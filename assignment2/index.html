About Me

I am Joseph Campbell's Hero of a Thousand Faces. I have degrees in biochemistry, religion, and anthropology, and yet my fire for coding lit through all of my professional history.

I taught myself to code in order to bring Native American culture to worldwide attention. This lifetime ambition began in 1996 with Edgar's Mesoamerican Art Page, and I am applying my current knowledge of PHP and SQL languages to develop what could be an unprecedented, open-source encyclopedia of Native American cultures. While I am in Austin, I look forward to presenting this prospectus to the University of Texas departments of anthropology and linguistics, as well as its Archive of Indigenous Languages of Latin America.
